## Introduction

At Berkia, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you access our SaaS platform and related services. We encourage you to read this policy carefully. By using our services, you agree to the practices described herein.

## Information We Collect

### Personal Information

- **Account Information**: Your email address, name, and password for account creation and management.
- **Billing Information**: Payment details and billing address for processing transactions.
- **Profile Information**: Company name, job title, and profile picture for enhancing user experience.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with our platform, including features used and navigation patterns.
- **Communication Data**: Records of messages and correspondence with our support team.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Technical Data**: Information such as IP address, browser type, and device information for troubleshooting and analytics.
- **Usage Statistics**: Data regarding pages visited, features used, and time spent on the platform.
- **Cookies and Similar Technologies**: Data collected through cookies for session management and preference settings.
- **Log Data**: Server logs, error reports, and performance data to improve service quality.

## How We Use Your Information

### Essential Operations

- Providing, maintaining, and improving our services.
- Processing transactions and managing accounts.
- Authenticating user identities and responding to support requests.

### Platform Improvement

- Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience.
- Developing and testing new features.
- Conducting research and analysis to inform future updates.

### Communications

- Sending system notifications and service updates.
- Marketing communications (only with your consent).
- Responding to support inquiries and providing security alerts.

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Location

- Data is stored in primary data centers located in the EU, with backup servers in secure locations to ensure data integrity and availability.

### Security Measures

- We implement SSL/TLS encryption, regular security audits, access control systems, database encryption, and routine backups to protect your information.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Third-Party Service Providers

- We may share your information with third-party service providers, including payment processors, analytics services, email service providers, and customer support tools to enhance service functionality.

### Legal Requirements

- We may disclose your information in response to court orders, legal obligations, government requests, or to protect rights in emergency situations.

## Your Rights and Choices

### Access Rights

- You have the right to view and download your personal data, request corrections, and update your preferences.

### Control Options

- You can opt-out of marketing communications, manage cookie preferences, request account deletion, and control data portability.

## Children's Privacy

- Our services are not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children and will delete any such information if discovered.

## International Data Transfers

### Data Protection Measures

- We comply with EU-US Privacy Shield standards and utilize standard contractual clauses to ensure data protection during international transfers.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

### Update Process

- We regularly review our policy and will notify you of any changes. Significant changes will be communicated via email with a notice period of 30 days.
