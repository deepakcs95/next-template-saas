## Introduction

At Berkia, we strive to ensure your satisfaction with our SaaS templates. This refund policy outlines our guidelines for refunds and cancellations. We aim to be transparent and fair while maintaining the sustainability of our business.

## Refund Eligibility

### 14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

- All new purchases (individual templates or bundles) are eligible for a 14-day money-back guarantee.
- Full refund within the first 14 days, no questions asked.
- Applies to first-time purchases only.
- Refund requests must be submitted within the eligible period.

### Pro-rated Refunds

- Available for bundled purchases if only part of the bundle is used.
- Calculated based on unused portion of the templates.
- Subject to review and approval.
- Administrative fees may apply for processing.
- Excludes any additional fees for usage or add-ons.

### Non-Refundable Items

- Setup fees
- Custom development work
- Third-party integrations
- Implementation services
- Usage-based charges
- Add-on services

## How to Request a Refund

### Process Steps

1. Log into your Berkia account.
2. Navigate to Billing Settings.
3. Click "Request Refund."
4. Complete the refund request form.
5. Submit any supporting documentation if required.

### Required Information

- Account details
- Purchase date
- Reason for refund
- Transaction ID
- Preferred refund method

## Processing Timeline

### Standard Refunds

- Review within 2 business days.
- Processing time: 5-10 business days.
- Credit card refunds: 3-5 business days.
- Bank transfers: 5-7 business days.
- PayPal: 1-3 business days.

### Special Cases

- Disputed charges: Up to 30 days.
- Bank investigations: 15-30 days.
- International transfers: 7-14 days.
- Complex cases handled on a case-by-case basis.

## Cancellation Policy

### Template Cancellation

- Cancel your subscription or template purchase anytime through your account settings.
- No cancellation fees apply.
- Access to the template continues until the end of the billing period.
- No partial refunds for the month of cancellation.
- Data retention for 30 days post-cancellation.

### Auto-Renewal

- Auto-renewal will be disabled upon cancellation.
- No additional charges incurred after cancellation.
- A confirmation email will be sent to confirm the cancellation.
- Option to reactivate templates or subscriptions available.
- Grace period for reactivation is provided.

## Special Circumstances

### Enterprise Accounts

- Custom agreements and terms may apply.
- Please contact your account manager for specific details.
- Volume-based discounts and terms can be negotiated.

### Technical Issues

- Full refunds available if the purchased template is unusable due to technical issues.
- Documented issues must be provided for review.
- A support ticket reference is required.
- An attempt to resolve the issue must be made before requesting a refund.
- Evaluation will be done on a case-by-case basis.

## Dispute Resolution

### Process

1. Submit a formal dispute request.
2. An investigation will be initiated.
3. Relevant documentation will be reviewed.
4. A decision will be communicated to the user.
5. An appeal process is available for contested decisions.

### Appeal Process

- Users have 30 days to appeal a decision.
- A written explanation must be provided for the appeal.
- Additional evidence may be submitted.
- Final decision after appeal is binding.
- Management review will be conducted as necessary.

## Exceptions

### Force Majeure

Refunds may not be granted due to circumstances beyond our control, including:

- Natural disasters
- Service outages
- Third-party failures
- Regulatory changes
- Emergency situations

### Abuse Prevention

Refunds may be denied in cases of:

- Multiple refund requests
- Fraudulent activity
- Violation of terms of service
- Misuse of services
- Account sharing

## Additional Terms

### Currency

- All refunds will be processed in the original currency used for the purchase.
- Exchange rates may apply at the time of the refund.
- Any bank fees incurred will be deducted from the refund amount.
- Processing fees related to the transaction are non-refundable.
- Local tax regulations will be adhered to.

### Documentation

- Copies of purchase receipts will be provided upon request.
- Refund confirmations will be sent via email.
- Transaction records will be maintained for your reference.
- Credit notes will be issued for applicable refunds.
- Tax documentation will be updated as necessary.

## Service Continuation

### After Cancellation

- Read-only access to the purchased templates will be provided for 30 days.
- Data export options are available.
- Access to features will be limited post-cancellation.
- Support services may be restricted.
- A formal account closure process will be initiated after the retention period.

### Data Handling

- A 30-day data retention period will be observed after cancellation.
- Tools for exporting data will be provided to users.
- Secure deletion processes will be implemented for all user data.
- Backups will be removed according to compliance standards.
